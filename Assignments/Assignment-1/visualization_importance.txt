Histogram made on math's score gives distribution of students with respect to score. With this we can decides how many comes under a grade.
Bar plot, displays the average math score for each gender, allowing us to compare performance between male and female students.
Scatter plot on reading and writing scores gives the trend between these two values of student. We can see obnormalities like if a student good at reading but bad at writing.
Box plot, this box plot illustrates how math scores are distributed across different parental education levels. It provides insights into score spread and potential outliers for each category.
Heatmap of the correlation matrix shows the relationships between the math, reading, and writing scores. It allows us to quickly see which subjects are most strongly correlated with each other.
